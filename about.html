The Bon-Appetite Story

Bon-Appetite is a food ordering website made exclusively for the students of Rayat Bahra University to pre-order food. our mission is to make a single food point for the students of Rayat Bahra Unversity from where they can pre-order their favourite food available on all the food courts in the Univeristy. Our main motive is to save their time so that after pre-ordering food they can just visit the food court and can take away their order.

Our Founding

Bon-appetite is founded Rohan Kumar and Mehakdeep Kaur (Students of b.tech CSE). Our food ordering website is founded with a mission to provide a seamless and convenient way for food lovers of our University to order their favorite dishes from all the food points available in the University campus. We believe that everyone should have access to delicious and affordable food without the hassle of waiting in the queue for their order preparation. With our platform, you can browse menu offerings from all the food courts of our University, make a call to place your order, all from the comfort of their device. Our goal is to make food ordering as effortless and enjoyable as possible. We hope to serve as a trusted companion for your hunger needs and a place where you can find your next food adventure.
